Garri

Garri is an easy summer snack as it is cold and refreshing

Garri is soaked in condensed milk and can be served with peanuts

Ingredients

  • Garri
  • Condensed milk
  • Sugar
  • If wanted you can add peanuts

Steps

  1. First wash the garri thoroughly.
  2. Add condensed milk to the garri in the cup.
  3. Add sugar to your liking to the garri.
  4. Stir well
