Plantain Chips

Plantain Chips are snacks that west african people love especially young children

Plantain Chips are an easy snack that consist of only three ingredient

Ingredients

  • 2 green plantains
  • Vegetable oil
  • Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Cut of the ends of each plantain.
  2. Peel plantains.
  3. Cut plantains in circle shape
  4. Line a rimmed baking sheet with a wire rack. Fill a large, deep
    cast iron or stainless steel skillet halfway with oil. Set over
    medium-high heat until the oil reaches 175Â°C. Working in batches
    and avoiding crowding the pan, add plantain slices and
    fry, turning occasionally, until golden brown all over, about 4
    minutes total. Using a spider or slotted spoon, transfer plantains
    plantains to wire rack to drain. Season right away with salt. Repeat with
    remaining plantain slices.
